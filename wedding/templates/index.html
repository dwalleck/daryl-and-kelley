Attractions

Enjoy the sights of San Antonio. From Historic landmarks to green golf courses, a range of activites await your enjoyment.

The Alamo

First Spanish Mission in Texas. Site of a major battle for the independence of Texas from Mexico.

FREE Admission

The Riverwalk

The Riverwalk is a public park lined iwht restaurants, hotes, shopping and more. Cruise the river on a tour boat.

FREE Admission - Tour $8.25

Witte Museum

Exhibits include Ancient Texans, Texas Settlers, Dinosaurs and a Mummy.

$10 Adult Admission

Pearl Brewery

On the site of the old Pearl Brewery, the location now hosts food, shops and a year-round farmers market.

FREE Admission

San Antonio Zoo

See the animals and ride the Brackenridge Train. The San Antonio Zoo celebrates its 100 year anniversary this year.

$12 Zoo Adult Admission - Train $3.50

Six Flags Fiesta Texas

Roller coasters! Take advantage of the off season. Open weekends in September.

$45 Adult Admission

The Rim

Nordstrom Rack, Sack Fifth Avenue OFF 5th, TJ Maxx, Bass Pro Shop, and JC Penney are just a few of the shops.

FREE Admission

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema

Watch a movie while eating dinner and drinking a beer. Showtimes include a mix of classic and just released movies.
